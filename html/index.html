Frequently Asked Questions

Is this against LinkedIn's terms of service?

Nuomi.ai works just like a human would - we don't scrape or use LinkedIn's API. We simply automate the browser interaction that you would normally do manually.

Will employers know I'm using a bot?

No. Our bot applies exactly like a human would. There's no way for employers to tell the difference between our bot and a human applicant.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your computer. We never access or store your personal information on our servers.
